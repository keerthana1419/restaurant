Our Story

A Legacy of Flavor Since 1998

Bella Cucina was born from a simple dream — to bring the authentic warmth of Italian home cooking to the world. Founded in Milano by Chef Marco Rossi, our restaurant has been a gathering place for food lovers for over 25 years.

Every recipe has been passed down through generations, refined with modern techniques but never losing the soul of traditional Italian cuisine.
